+Aspergere+, %to besprinkle%.
* Aram sanguine aspergere. #Cic.#
Figuratively:
* Si illius comitatem tuæ gravitati asperseris, #Cic.#, if you season your gravity with the gentleness of his manners.
* Sales orationi aspergere. #Id.#
* Hæc aspersi, ut scires, &c. #Id.#, I have slightly touched that point, in order that you should know, &c.
+Rigare+, %to water, to bathe%.
* Fons circumjectos irrigat agros. #Q. Curt.#
* Rore rigare. #Cic.#
Figuratively:
* Rigare ingenia, #Cic.#, to enlighten minds.
